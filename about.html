ABOUT US
INK IT, born in the year 2018, was created initially for helping kids get better at understanding of English language. It then structured into Creative Writing and Reading. We helped several of students write better and comprehend topics. Each session dedicatedly works on areas from sentence formations, paragraphs structure, creative ideas, better vocabulary and new forms of writing.

⁃ Need of the hour
English has become a must for today’s kids. A strong foundation will help the child secure grades and help them in every situation from speaking confidently to understanding other subjects. All of the new boards focus greatly on this dynamic language and we proudly cater to all of the Boards.
⁃ Our Vision and Mission
Ink it, visions to see every child to connect with its inner self and express ideas freely. To create a future, where language is not a barrier to dreams.
